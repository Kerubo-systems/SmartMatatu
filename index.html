What is SmartMatatu?

SmartMatatu is an independent engineering research project exploring how technology can improve safety, accountability, comfort, and sustainability in Kenya's public transport system.

Why this survey?

Your responses will help us identify the most important challenges passengers face today and inform future prototype designs.
